fuzzydoDB (Fuzzy Domains Data Bases)

is a project initiated by professors of the Computer Science and Information Technology Department at Universidad Simón Bolívar whose development is currently undertaken by Computer Engineering students of the same institute.

The aim of the project is to specify, store and query databases with fuzzy attributes in the hopes it will ultimately enable the corroboration of theoretic results that have been attained in the field of study.
